    All you have to do is tap on the highlighted section enclosed by the yellow brackets, and you're done. Simply close the text scanner tool afterward.

  • How to utilize the dictation feature?

    Just tap the recording button and start dictating. We advise entering one sentence at a time to improve accuracy.
